Focus

This week we look at the dialogic elements in a core Islamic educational source, namely Hadith Jibrīl. We explore the relationship between Jibrīl (as) and Muhammad (saw) as a dialogic one. We consider in what ways Prophetic halaqah was dialogic. We begin to start thinking about the importance of dialogic engagement for transformative education. We introduce a range of Islamic educational terms and touch on their dialogic dimensions. We begin to start thinking about how to incorporate dialogic practices into our own teaching.

There is only one reading and a supplementary video summarising the reading for this week. Please do work through these as well as the Course Video. It may be useful to do this before watching the Course Video this week. Don’t forget to use your reflective journal template for this week to record your thoughts.
