Final Reflective Report:

Congratulations on having completed the course materials. You are now ready to write your reflective report which should be approximately 2000 words describing what you have learnt from the report and an evaluation of any changes to your classroom practice. You can use your weekly reflective journal notes to help you think about how to structure and write this report. The template below will also be useful to you. You can simply fill it in or if you have time, you can use it to craft a report. Completed reports should be sent via email to xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx. The deadline for submitting reports is xxxx xxxx.
