Focus

This week we think more about how our niyyah shapes our teaching and learn about the practical dimensions of halaqah. We briefly explore how teachers can give children time to develop their thinking on a key question and draw on their existing knowledge to learn from each other. We use a halaqah transcript to illustrate this through an example. We also look at how ground rules support dialogue and think about the ways we can develop ground rules for halaqah. Remember to make notes in your Week 5 Reflective Journal template as you work through the materials.
