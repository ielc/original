Do

  • Using the prompts on the Reflective Journal template, reflect on your relationships with your learners and on your own lifelong learning as an educator.
  • Reflect on the relationships you had with one of your previous teachers. What was the relationship like? How could it have been better?
  • Get in touch with a previous teacher of yours and find out how they are. How have they changed? Have they grown and developed? If you can’t do that, discuss these ideas with someone who will help you think about them.
